Privacy Policy — What Time To Run

Effective date: 27 June 2026

What Time To Run (“the app”) helps you choose a good time to go for a run based on the weather forecast. We respect your privacy. This policy explains what the app does and does not do with your data.

Data we collect

None. The app has no user accounts and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to us. We have no servers and receive no data from you.

Location

The app does not access your device’s GPS or location. You search for a place by typing its name. That name, and the coordinates returned for it, are sent to the Open-Meteo service to look up the weather forecast.

On-device storage

Your preferences (selected location, temperature/wind units, and time range) are saved locally on your device only and can be cleared by clearing the app’s data or uninstalling it.

Third-party service

The app uses Open-Meteo to provide geocoding and weather forecasts. Requests to Open-Meteo are subject to their terms and privacy practices: open-meteo.com/en/terms.

Analytics & advertising

The app contains no advertising, no analytics, and no third-party tracking.

Children

The app does not collect personal data and is suitable for users of all ages.

Changes

We may update this policy; any changes will be posted at this page.

Contact

For privacy questions, please use the developer contact details shown on the app’s Google Play store listing.
